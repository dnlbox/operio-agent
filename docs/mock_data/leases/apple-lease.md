# LEASE AGREEMENT — APPLE STORE (UNIT 108)

This Lease Agreement is entered into by and between **Operio Mall Properties Ltd.** (Landlord) and **Apple Canada Inc.** (Tenant) for the premises located at Unit 108, Sector A.

---

## Section 6. Maintenance and Triple-Net Liabilities

### Section 6.1 - Full HVAC Operational Liability
This agreement is a triple-net lease. The Tenant shall assume 100% of the cost and responsibility for the maintenance, repair, and replacement of all dedicated HVAC units, compressors, coils, heat pumps, and ducts serving Unit 108.
*   **Landlord Exemption:** Landlord is exempt from any HVAC maintenance or repair costs, regardless of the single repair event value.

### Section 6.2 - Structural Roof Membrane and Storefront Glass
The Tenant is responsible for repairing the structural roof membrane directly above Unit 108 if damage is related to HVAC weight or roof penetrations. The Tenant also assumes full liability for all storefront glazing, structural glass supports, and entryway glass doors.
