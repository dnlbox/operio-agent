# LEASE AGREEMENT — PUMA STORE (UNIT 107)

This Lease Agreement is entered into by and between **Operio Mall Properties Ltd.** (Landlord) and **Puma Canada Ltd.** (Tenant) for the premises located at Unit 107, Sector B.

---

## Section 8. Storefront Operations and Services

### Section 8.1 - Entrance Doors, Rolling Grilles, and Locks
The Tenant shall assume 100% operational and financial liability for the maintenance, repair, and replacement of all storefront entrance doors, locks, glass panels, rolling security grilles, and overhead gates.
*   **Security Systems:** Tenant is responsible for all shoplifting sensors and security alarms.

### Section 8.2 - Electrical Panels and Utility Feeders
The Landlord shall maintain main electrical risers and panels feeding Unit 107 up to the demarcation point. All sub-panels and branch wiring within the store boundary are the Tenant's responsibility.
