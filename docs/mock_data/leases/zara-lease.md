# LEASE AGREEMENT — ZARA STORE (UNIT 106)

This Lease Agreement is entered into by and between **Operio Mall Properties Ltd.** (Landlord) and **Zara Canada Inc.** (Tenant) for the premises located at Unit 106, Sector A.

---

## Section 14. Maintenance and Interior Systems

### Section 14.1 - Interior Plumbing and Lighting
The Tenant shall, at its own expense, maintain and repair all interior lighting fixtures, electrical wiring within the store boundary, and all internal plumbing fixtures serving Unit 106. The Tenant's financial liability for any single plumbing repair event is capped at **Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00)**. Any repair costs above this limit shall be assumed by the Landlord.

### Section 14.2 - Structural Integrity & Common Facilities
The Landlord shall maintain and repair the roof membrane, building foundations, exterior walls, and centralized common area air handling systems, except where damage is caused by Tenant operations.
