# LEASE AGREEMENT — ADIDAS STORE (UNIT 105)

This Lease Agreement is entered into by and between **Operio Mall Properties Ltd.** (Landlord) and **Adidas Canada Ltd.** (Tenant) for the premises located at Unit 105, Sector B.

---

## Section 11. Facilities and Mechanical Services

### Section 11.1 - Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC)
The Landlord shall maintain, repair, and replace the centralized HVAC system serving Unit 105. The Tenant is exempt from direct repair costs, provided that the Tenant does not obstruct intake vents or modify thermostat settings outside of authorized ranges.
*   **Tenant Obligation:** Tenant shall notify Landlord immediately of any temperature fluctuations. Tenant is not authorized to hire third-party HVAC contractors.

### Section 11.2 - Glazing and Storefront Glass
The Tenant shall assume 100% operational and financial liability for any cracks, chips, or breakage of the storefront window glass, regardless of cause.
