# Generac Generator Manual (Model-Protector)

## Section 8 - Emergency Power Systems

### Low Oil Pressure Lockout & Battery Faults
If the backup generator fails to crank during an auto-transfer event:
1. **Reset Low Oil Lockout:** Check oil level (verify it is within the crosshatch area of the dipstick). Press "Off" then "Enter" on the control board to clear the low oil pressure alarm code.
2. **Battery Charger Fault:** Inspect the battery charger output fuse. Verify battery voltage is above 12.6V DC. Clean corroded battery terminals.
3. **Manual Transfer Override:** If the automatic transfer switch (ATS) fails to slide, insert the manual operating handle and transition the switch load manually.
