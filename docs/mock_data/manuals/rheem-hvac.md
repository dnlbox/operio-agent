# Rheem HVAC Classic Manual

## Section 3 - Low Suction Pressure Troubleshooting

### Troubleshooting Coils Freezing
Low suction pressure causes the evaporator coils to freeze. Perform the following checks:
1. **Dirty Air Filters:** Check the air filters. Clean or replace filters if airflow is restricted.
2. **Refrigerant Charge:** Check if refrigerant charge is low (potential leak). Measure suction line pressure and compare against the ambient temperature chart.
3. **Blower Motor:** Verify the blower motor is running at target RPM. Replace blower belts if slipping is detected.
