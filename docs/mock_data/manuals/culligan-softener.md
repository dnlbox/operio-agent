# Culligan Water Softener Manual (Model-HE)

## Section 5 - Hard Water Troubleshooting

### Salt Bridge and Regeneration
If the water quality test reveals hard water downstream of the softener:
1. **Clear Salt Bridge:** Inspect the brine tank. If a hard crust or "salt bridge" has formed above the water level, break it manually using a clean tool.
2. **Initiate Regeneration:** Press and hold the "Regen" button for 3 seconds to trigger an immediate manual regeneration cycle.
3. **Bypass Valve Check:** Ensure the bypass valve is set to "Service" and not "Bypass".
