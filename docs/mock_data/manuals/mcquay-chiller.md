# McQuay Chiller Manual (Model-WSC)

## Section 4 - Flow Failures and Alarms

### Low Water Flow Lockout
If the chiller triggers a "Low Water Flow" alarm:
1. **Verify Water Pump:** Verify that the evaporator water pump is running and delivering target PSI.
2. **Inspect Strainers:** Check the inlet water strainer. Remove debris or silt that may block flow.
3. **Flow Switch Calibration:** Clean the paddle flow switch and verify the electrical continuity of the contacts when flow is active.
4. **Condenser Tube Maintenance:** Clean the condenser tubes to prevent scale buildup.
