# Kone Escalator Manual (Model-TravelMaster)

## Section 7 - Escalator Safety Circuits

### Step Chain Tension Adjustment & Comb Switch Reset
If the escalator shuts down and reports error code E-10:
1. **Verify Step Chain Tension:** Check step chain tension indicators at the bottom landing. Adjust the tension bolt if the chain slack exceeds 5mm.
2. **Comb Plate Switch Test:** Inspect the comb plate switches at both landings. Clear any obstructions (such as gravel or debris) caught in the comb segments.
3. **Emergency Brake Reset:** Reset the mechanical emergency brake handle inside the pit control box before restarting the unit.
