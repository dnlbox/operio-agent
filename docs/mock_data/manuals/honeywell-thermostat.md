# Honeywell Thermostat T6 Pro Manual

## Section 2 - Setup and Operation

### Battery Replacement & Calibration
If the thermostat display is blank or reports low battery warnings:
1. **Remove Cover:** Pull the thermostat body gently away from the wall mounting plate.
2. **Replace Batteries:** Insert 2 AA alkaline batteries.
3. **Calibrate Sensor:** If the displayed temperature is incorrect, access the Advanced Menu by pressing and holding the Menu button for 5 seconds. Navigate to Option 12 (Temperature Offset) and adjust the sensor reading.
4. **Wiring Check:** Verify that the Y1 (Cooling) and W1 (Heating) wires are securely connected to their terminal blocks.
